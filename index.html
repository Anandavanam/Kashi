About the hotel
Where bliss meets heritage

Nestled in the heart of the world's oldest living city, Anandavanam blends timeless Indian hospitality with modern luxury. Just minutes from the Kashi Viswanath Temple.

Every room is a sanctuary — plush teal-upholstered headboards, warm wooden floors, premium linens, and every contemporary convenience crafted to make your Varanasi journey truly memorable.
